Data Science as a Service

Machine learning can provide powerful ways to predict, personalize, alert, and make decisions. Building machine learning applications can be difficult and expensive and there needs to be robust data to drive effective predictions. Through our DSaaS (data science as a service) arm, MLDB.ai's data science team provides data and infrastructure audits and recommendations, isolates the type of personalized decisions you need, and develops applications on top of MLDB, the machine learning database, that addresses your needs for predictions and decisions with a meaningful impact on your business.

Case Studies:

Online community management product realized that human moderators were spending most of their time reviewing content that was falsely reported as bullying. MLDB.ai's data scientists developed a toxic content predictive model using NLP techniques that screened reports so that moderators only had to review content that was too close to call. This resulted in a 50% reduction in moderator workload while letting though only 5% of toxic messages. This is a great example of human augmentation.

Flash Retailer sending 3 million emails per day to customers with 30 different products, were segmenting users into audiences for content order. We developed a personalized e-mail offering for each user based on their behavior on their site which garnered a 20% increase in revenue.

Large telco needed to serve the highest performing creative on their ad inventory. We developed a yield optimization solution to personalize the creative delivered for ad serving on their owned & operated inventory, resulting in 50% lift on revenue.

Large Electronics Retailer needed to understand the difference between converting and non-converting users. We conducted analyses into the drivers of customer behavior, and created deep analytic reports to generate insights not available in their DMP, which led to product creative adjustments and increased response rates on ad campaigns.
